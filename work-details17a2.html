Assignment 2

Assignment information

  • Category: Unit 17
  • Assignment Start: 11 January, 2023
  • Assignment End: 17 February, 2023
  • Grade: Distinction

In this assignment, we had to design, create, test and review two multimedia products for students aged 16 and over:
1. A multimedia presentation, including navigation keys that will be displayed on PC’s so students can interactively look at what’s on offer within the computing department at college
2. A multimedia advert that will be shown, on loop on the interactive whiteboards in all the computing classrooms. The advert must run for a minimum of 20 seconds.

The assignment includes:
• Description of purpose and audience/user requirements for each product
• Alternative solutions
• A table of the assets used
• A test plan for each product
• Functional multimedia products
• Self-review
• Peer review
• Alterations with justifications
